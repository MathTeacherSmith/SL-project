An International Baccalaureate (IB) School is a school authorized to offer one or more of the International Baccalaureate programs. The IB is an internationally recognized educational foundation that provides a challenging curriculum aimed at developing students' intellectual, personal, emotional, and social skills for a globalized world.

About Me

I went to an IB School from the 10th to 12th grade. I believe that my life would be different mostly because it really prepared me for college and made the next 5 years of my life easier.

I was TERRIBLE at writing essays. The IB school mostly prepared me to write essays at the college level.

Biggest Strugles

One of my biggest struggles in the IB Program was completing the extended essay. I wrote about the golden ratio (approximately 1.618).

I also struggled with community service. I was never part of a program that included service and I also lived far away from the school so it was hard to attend events outside of school time.
